# 📊 Analytics Discount Strategy

Analytics project focused on pricing strategy, revenue optimization, and data-driven decision making.

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project analyzes the impact of discount strategies on sales performance, profitability, and customer behavior.

The goal is to identify how discounting affects revenue and margin, and to develop data-driven recommendations for optimizing pricing strategy.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Companies often use discounts to increase sales, but:

- Discounts can reduce profitability
- Poorly structured discounting leads to revenue leakage
- The impact on customer behavior is often unclear

👉 This project answers:
- How do discounts affect sales and revenue?
- Which product segments are most sensitive to pricing changes?
- What is the optimal discount strategy to balance growth and profitability?

---

## 📊 Key Insights

- Discount strategy has a **direct but non-linear impact** on revenue
- Certain product segments show **high price elasticity**
- Over-discounting leads to **margin erosion without proportional sales growth**
- Optimized discount levels can improve **overall profitability**

---

## 🧠 Methodology

- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Discount vs revenue correlation analysis
- Segment-based performance evaluation
- Business-driven interpretation of results

---

## 📈 Tools & Technologies

- Python

---

## 💡 Key Takeaway

This analysis demonstrates that discounting is not just a sales tool, but a **strategic lever for profitability optimization**.
Proper discount calibration allows companies to:
- Increase revenue efficiency
- Protect margins
- Improve long-term customer value
